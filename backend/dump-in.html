AI Overview

Intraday stocks are shares bought and sold within the same trading day to profit from short-term price movements, with all positions closed before the market closes to avoid overnight risks
. Popular choices often include highly liquid, high-volume stocks like Tata Consultancy Services, Bharti Airtel, and Coal India. Key strategies involve technical analysis to navigate high-risk, fast-paced market fluctuations. 
Commonly Traded Intraday Stocks 
  • Top Nifty 50 Examples: Tata Consultancy Services Ltd, Bharti Airtel Ltd, Coal India Ltd, Power Grid Corporation.
  • High Activity/Volatility Examples: RPSG Ventures, HEG, Brigade Enterprises, Oil & Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC).
  • Stocks with High Volume: GUJALKALI, PCBL, LT, GODFRYPHLP.
